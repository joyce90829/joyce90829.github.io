AMood

Project Introduction

Have you ever wished to start off your morning in a different way rather than wake up in the alarm and hurry in every minute to work? How about being woken up by the nice smell of freshly made coffee and breakfast? AMood is a mobile app designed to make your morning experience more enjoyable.

My Problem in the Morning

The morning alarm sometimes can be so unpleasant and loud. All you want to do is turning it off and going back to sleep. So you snooze the alarm again and againâ€¦ After half an hour, finally you get up.

Goal of Design

The goal of this project is not only just to make a coffee alarm machine but also to make our morning experience more enjoyable thus we can be more productive.

My Inspiration
