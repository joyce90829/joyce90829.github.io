Seattle Travel Guide

Project Introduction

Seattle Travel Guide is a resource for young backpackers who like traveling around the world. It includes the basic traveling info such as transportation, where to go, where to stay and restaurants. It is a bifold brochure, which is easy for users to carry around.
(It is inspired by the travel guides on Hostelworld.com)
